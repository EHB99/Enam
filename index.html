About

Interdisciplinary MRI physicist and biomedical engineer integrating electromagnetic modeling, system-level engineering, and AI-based analysis to advance ultra-fast and quantitative MRI. My work spans diffusion biophysics, multi-omics, explainable AI, and reproducible biomarker development for translational imaging and population health.

Research interests

  • MRI hardware and signal modeling; quantitative imaging and motion correction
  • Computational biophysics and multi-omics integration
  • AI-driven biomarker discovery; radiogenomics; explainable AI
  • Causal inference for harmonization across field strengths and sites
  • Population-level imaging analytics and One Health modeling

Interdisciplinary focus areas

  • Computational Biophysics & Multi-Omics: physics-informed modeling of tissue microstructure and pathways
  • AI-Driven Imaging & Radiogenomics: deep learning + XAI for oncology, pain, neurodegeneration
  • Biomedical Engineering & Functional Imaging: adaptive hardware, diffusion, FEA/MRE biomechanics
  • Public Health Analytics: causal harmonization and risk prediction for population outcomes
